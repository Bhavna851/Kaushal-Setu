Welcome to Kaushal Setu
Kaushal Setu is a smart internship recommendation platform developed to help students discover relevant internship opportunities based on their educational qualifications, technical skills, and preferred location. The system matches student profiles with suitable internships and provides direct access to official application portals through a simple, secure, and user-friendly interface.

Smart Recommendations

Recommends suitable internships by matching a student's education, technical skills, and preferred location with available opportunities.

Resume-based profile

Allows students to upload their resume and maintain a complete profile for faster and more accurate internship recommendations.

Verified Internship Listings

Displays internships from official company career portals and trusted recruitment platforms with direct application links.

Simple & User-Friendly

Clean, responsive, and easy-to-use interface that helps students explore, apply, and manage internship opportunities with ease.
